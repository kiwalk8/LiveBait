Welcome to Lakeside!

Started by Uncle Howard in 1990, the General Store and Bait Shop has been a staple for the Lakeside community for 33 years. You can still find Howard's friendly face at the counter, so stop by sometime to say hello! We have the products and services to ensure your trip to Lakeside is smooth sailing.
